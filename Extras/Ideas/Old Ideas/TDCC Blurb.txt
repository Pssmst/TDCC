"Celeste" is a video game in the platformer genre created by Extremely OK Games. The game follows the story of a woman who climbs Celeste Mountain in an attempt to berid herself of her own anxiety and depression and discover her own identity.

The Dogs Climb Celeste is a fan-made novel based on the video game "Celeste" in the form of an RPG. This book follows the story of a man who climbs Celeste Mountain with his friends in an attempt to berid himself of his own shame and destroy his false identities.
